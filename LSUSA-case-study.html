Overview

Legal Serve USA is a Florida-based company specializing in professional process serving, skip tracing, and affidavits of diligent search for legal documents like summons and subpoenas. They operate across key locations including Orlando, Miami, Tampa, Tallahassee, and Plantation, using mobile apps for real-time updates and GPS-stamped proofs to ensure accuracy and compliance. The firm emphasizes efficiency, reliability, and technology to help legal professionals meet deadlines without hassle.


The Challenge

Legal Serve USA, a B2C service platform, was struggling to generate consistent organic traffic and qualified leads. Despite having a functional website, visibility in search engines was limited, and user engagement remained low. Key performance indicators such as keyword rankings, backlinks, and traffic volume were underperforming—resulting in missed revenue opportunities.


The Solution

  1. Technical SEO & Site Optimization

    2. I started by conducting a full technical audit of the website, identifying and fixing crawlability issues, improving page speed, and optimizing mobile performance. This ensured search engines could properly index the site while also enhancing the overall user experience.

  2. Content & Keyword Strategy

    3. I developed a targeted keyword strategy focused on high-intent, low-competition search terms. Then, I created and optimized content around these keywords to capture relevant traffic and improve rankings for core service queries.

  3. Authority Building & Backlinks

    4. I implemented a strategic link-building campaign, acquiring high-quality backlinks from relevant and authoritative sources. This strengthened the site’s domain authority and significantly improved its ability to rank for competitive keywords.


The Results

  • Organic Traffic Growth: Increased to 480 monthly visitors, representing a significant lift from a previously low baseline.
  • Keyword Rankings: Expanded to 23 ranking keywords, improving search visibility across core service terms.
  • Backlink Profile: Grew to 119 backlinks, strengthening domain authority and trust signals.
  • Engagement Metrics: Improved on-site engagement, with users spending more time interacting with service pages.
  • Conversion Uplift: Estimated 200–300% increase in conversions, driven by higher intent traffic and improved UX.

The Outcome

Within a short optimization cycle, Legal Serve USA transformed its digital presence from low visibility to a steadily growing acquisition channel. Organic search became a reliable driver of both traffic and conversions, reducing dependency on paid channels and improving overall marketing ROI. The combination of technical improvements, strategic content, and authority building positioned the brand for long-term, scalable growth.
